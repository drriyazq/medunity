# Play Console — Content Rating (IARC) Answers

Target rating: **Mature 17+ (USK 16)** likely, due to medical/anatomical content references in clinical posts. The walkthrough below answers honestly. The rating itself does not block access — Target Audience is set separately to **18+ only**.

---

## Category selection
**Reference apps** — "Reference, News, or Educational" (MedUnity is a professional networking + reference app for verified clinicians).

---

## Violence
- Does your app contain violence? **No**
- Cartoon / fantasy violence? **No**
- Realistic violence? **No** *(Note: SOS categories include "clinic under threat / mob violence" as a free-text emergency type; this is a labelling field, not depicted content.)*
- Sexual violence? **No**

## Sexuality
- Sexual content? **No**
- Nudity? **No**

## Language
- Profanity? **No** *(no curated content; user posts inside circles are moderated by the circle admin and the MedUnity admin team.)*
- Crude humour? **No**

## Controlled substances
- Alcohol references? **No**
- Tobacco references? **No**
- Drug references? **Possibly — clinical context only.** Doctors discuss prescription medications, controlled substances (anaesthetics, opioids), and dosage in case threads. This is professional clinical reference, not recreational depiction.

## Gambling
- Simulated gambling? **No**
- Real-money gambling? **No**

## Horror & fear
- Horror themes? **No**
- Blood or gore? **Possibly — clinical context only.** Surgical photographs, post-extraction images, and trauma documentation may be uploaded to circle posts or marketplace listings (e.g. equipment used in oral surgery). Verified doctors only. No gratuitous depiction.

## Miscellaneous

### Does your app share users' location?
**Yes — within the app.** A doctor's clinic location is visible to other verified doctors on the SOS, consultant, and circles maps. Location is **never** shared with advertisers or third parties.

### Does your app allow users to interact or exchange content with other users?
**Yes** — verified doctors only, inside circles (posts, comments), consultant booking notes, marketplace inquiries, and vendor reviews. All UGC is moderated; verified-only network reduces abuse vectors significantly.

### Does your app collect or share users' personal information?
**Yes** — see Data Safety form. Not shared with third parties.

### Does your app contain advertising?
**No**

### Does your app allow users to purchase digital content?
**No**

### Are there unmoderated user-generated videos / images / text?
**Moderated.** Posts/comments can be flagged by any verified doctor; circle admins and MedUnity admins remove violating content. Vendor flags surface to admins for review.

### Does your app enable in-app communication (chat, VoIP, forum) between users?
**Yes** — circle posts and comments (forum-style). No real-time chat or VoIP.

### Does your app permit the download of digital content?
**No** — content is server-rendered. Marketplace listing photos can be saved by the user via long-press, same as any image in Android.

---

## Expected rating
**Mature 17+** in regions where the IARC questionnaire flags "drug references" or "blood" for clinical apps. Some regions return **Teen / 12+**. The rating display is informational; the **Target Audience = 18+** setting is the binding age gate.

---

## Notes for the reviewer
- All clinical content is gated behind the manual verification step. No general public can see surgical images, prescription discussions, or clinic locations.
- The "clinic under threat / mob violence" SOS category is a free-text label for an emergency dispatch button, not depicted violent content.
- Drug references are limited to clinical dosage discussions among verified prescribers.
- This is a professional medical app, not a consumer health app.
