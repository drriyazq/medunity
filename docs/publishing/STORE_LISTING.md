## Full description (4000 characters max)

```
MedUnity is a private, verified-only network for medical professionals in India. Doctors, dentists, surgeons, anesthesiologists, and visiting consultants connect with peers around them — for emergencies, second opinions, equipment sharing, and on-demand cover.

VERIFIED PROFESSIONALS ONLY
Every account is reviewed by the MedUnity admin team before access is granted. You upload your medical registration certificate; we verify it against the relevant council before unlocking the app. Until then, no features are accessible — keeping the network trusted by design.

WHAT YOU GET

• SOS — One-tap emergency dispatch
  Press the red SOS button when you face a medical emergency, mob threat, legal escalation, or need urgent clinical assistance. Nearby verified doctors receive a high-priority push within seconds. Whoever taps "On my way" appears on your live map.

• Local Doctor Circles
  Geo-anchored groups for doctors within 1–2 km of you. Discuss complex cases, share local resources, organise community events. Posts and comments stay inside the circle — they never leak to patients or the public web.

• Consultant Network
  Visiting endodontists, oral surgeons, anesthesiologists, and other specialists toggle Available / Busy. Clinics nearby see them on a map and can request urgent procedures (RCT, surgical extraction, sedation). Two-way reviews build trust over time. Zero commission — MedUnity only facilitates the connection.

• Equipment Co-Purchase + Marketplace
  Pool funds with neighbouring clinics to buy expensive equipment (intraoral scanners, surgical kits, OPG machines) and share scheduled time. Or list your surplus equipment to other verified doctors only — never to the open public.

• Practice Support + Brownie Points
  Going on holiday or stuck in a personal emergency? Request peer cover for urgent appointments. Power outage in your clinic? Borrow a neighbour's operatory. Every act of help earns Brownie Points on a regional leaderboard recognising the most generous contributors.

• Verified Vendor + Lab Directory
  Crowd-sourced directory of dental labs, equipment dealers, and material suppliers — only doctor-rated, with anti-duplication merging and a fraud-flag button.

PRIVACY BY DESIGN

• Verified-only access — no patients, no public, no recruiters.
• Your phone number is used solely to verify ownership during sign-in.
• Location is sampled only for SOS broadcasts, your clinic location, and consultant maps — never tracked in the background.
• No advertising. No personalised data sales. No third-party data brokers.
• All API traffic is HTTPS / TLS 1.2+. Local tokens are encrypted with AES-256 in the Android Keystore.

WHO IT IS FOR
Licensed medical professionals aged 18+ practising in India. Verification fields cover MBBS, BDS, MD, MS, MDS, BAMS, BHMS, and DNB registrations across the National Medical Commission, Dental Council of India, and individual state councils.

CONTACT
For verification queries, account issues, or grievances: drdentalmail@gmail.com
```
